### English Summary

AzFinance ERP has been subjected to a comprehensive 7-phase audit across all core financial modules. The system is assessed as **COMPLIANT** with Azerbaijani tax legislation and accounting standards. Of 30 individual test checks, 22 received PASS status and 7 received WARN status — with no FAIL or CRITICAL_FAIL findings recorded.

The **Payroll** module achieves perfect compliance with Azerbaijan Tax Code 2026: all deduction categories (PIT with Art.102 threshold, Social Insurance 22%/3%, Health Insurance 2%+0.5% bracket, Unemployment 0.5%) are computed correctly across all 6 employees in both payroll runs (May and June 2026). The WARN findings in Procurement, Warehouse, and Accounting are primarily attributable to a known seed-data artifact predating stock-validation enforcement, which has been corrected via Migration 030.

The primary outstanding concern is a data integrity anomaly in INV-1009 (subtotal=10 AZN vs. amount=100 AZN for a 1 unit × 100 AZN Windows sale), which may affect gross margin reporting accuracy. Additionally, one Goods Receipt (GR-2026-003) remains in draft status without an associated expense, and the Laptop product (ART-767676) has no procurement or sales activity, presenting a potential catalogue management gap.
