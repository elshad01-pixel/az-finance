### ENGLISH SUMMARY

AzFinance ERP has been subjected to a comprehensive 7-phase audit with 30 individual tests executed. The system demonstrates strong overall compliance with Azerbaijani tax law and accounting standards for 2026.

**Procurement (Phase 1):** The full procure-to-pay workflow (PR→PO→GR→Expense) is operational with 10 PRs, 11 POs, and 11 GRs. Known data corrections (Migration 030, 032, 034) have been properly applied. Minor issues include zero-value PRs and a non-standard payment_terms format.

**Warehouse (Phase 2):** FIFO batch tracking is correctly implemented with 5 product batches tied to GRs. Stock movement logs exist, but unit_cost=null on two OUT movements (DEL-004, DEL-005) and missing reference_id linkages represent technical gaps in traceability.

**Sales (Phase 3):** All 5 sales orders are delivered with auto-generated invoices. FIFO COGS calculations are correct across all deliveries. Historical data correction for DEL-2026-002 (Kreslo oversell) has been properly handled via migration.

**Accounting (Phase 4):** All 13 invoices show exactly 18% VAT — zero discrepancies. Expense categorization is sound. COGS values match FIFO calculations throughout.

**Payroll (Phase 5):** Perfect compliance across all 6 employees for both payroll runs. PIT with Art.102 deduction (200 AZN where gross ≤ 2,500 AZN), social insurance (22%/3%), health insurance (2%+0.5% tiered), and unemployment (0.5%) are all calculated correctly to the decimal.

**Reports (Phase 6):** Gross margin of 78.15% is healthy and correctly computed. P&L totals are consistent with underlying data. Gap identified: manual invoices with stock products do not trigger COGS calculation.

**Tax Compliance (Phase 7):** Company is VAT-registered and correctly applies 18% VAT. Total invoiced revenue of 210,280 AZN exceeds the 200,000 AZN threshold — registration is in place. Tax settings are properly configured. Minor discrepancy: employee_count=7 vs. 6 active employees in the employees table.
