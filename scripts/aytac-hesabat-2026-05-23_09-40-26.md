**IN ENGLISH — EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:**
AzFinance ERP underwent a comprehensive 7-phase audit on 23 May 2026. The system demonstrates significant operational gaps requiring immediate remediation. The Procure-to-Pay workflow functions at a basic level, but critical data integrity failures exist: negative warehouse stock (−3 units Kreslo), a 1,000-unit phantom stock discrepancy in products vs warehouse tables for Windows, and a missing product batch record for Kreslo preventing FIFO costing. In the Sales cycle, delivery DEL-2026-002 carries COGS=0 instead of the correct 120 AZN, causing gross margin reports to overstate profitability by 66.7 percentage points. Accounting records show 9 of 10 invoices with no VAT applied (vat_applied=false), representing a systemic VAT non-compliance risk of approximately 15,978 AZN in uncalculated VAT. The payroll module is the highest-performing component: all 6 employees pass Azerbaijan 2026 deduction verification (PIT, social insurance at 22%+3%, health insurance with 2%/0.5% bracket, unemployment at 0.5% each side) with zero discrepancy. Tax compliance review reveals that at the current revenue run-rate (~599K AZN annualized), the company will breach the 200,000 AZN VAT registration threshold by approximately August 2026, yet vat_registered=false in tax_settings.
