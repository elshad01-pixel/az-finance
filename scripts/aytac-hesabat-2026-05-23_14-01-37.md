### English

AzFinance ERP has been subjected to a comprehensive 7-phase audit covering Procurement (Procure-to-Pay), Warehouse (FIFO), Sales (Order-to-Cash), Accounting, Payroll, Reporting, and Tax Compliance modules as of 23 May 2026.

**The Payroll module** achieved perfect scores: all 6 employees' deductions were verified against Azerbaijan's 2026 tax legislation with zero discrepancies. PIT with Art.102 tiered deduction logic (200 AZN relief only for gross ≤ 2,500 AZN at main workplace), social insurance (22% employer, 3% employee), tiered health insurance (2% up to 2,500 AZN + 0.5% above), and unemployment insurance (0.5% each) — all computed correctly. This represents a strong certification result for the system's tax engine.

**The Procurement workflow** is fully functional: the PR→PO→GR→Expense chain executed correctly for 7 purchase requests, with automatic accrual-basis expense creation working as designed. A minor gap was identified: GR-2026-003 remains in 'draft' status while a duplicate GR-2026-004 was confirmed for the same PO — creating a risk of duplicate expense accrual if the draft is later confirmed.

**Warehouse and Sales** modules operate correctly with FIFO batch costing. The Kreslo stock correction (Migration 030) has been properly applied. Invoice VAT is exactly 18% across all 10 invoices. The cumulative invoiced revenue of 99,980 AZN remains below the 200,000 AZN VAT threshold, though the company is already voluntarily VAT-registered. One structural concern: PO-2026-003 generated a 100,000 AZN single expense entry which dominates the P&L and creates a reported net loss of approximately 47,911.5 AZN for the period — this warrants management review.
